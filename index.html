About

Rofnet (AS402164) is a personal homelab network used to experiment with IP routing, BGP announcements, and basic network operations. Outages and maintenance may occur without notice.

Goals

Learn by doing: BGP policy, filtering, monitoring, and safe operations.

Scope

Multi-homed anycast with upstream transit via Neptune Networks and Vultr. No transit offered to third parties.

Services

Self-hosted services and labs (details intentionally minimal).
